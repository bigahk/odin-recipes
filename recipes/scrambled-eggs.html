Scrabled Eggs

A dish anyone can make, but not one that people fully understand!

Scrambled eggs are eaten all around the world. Different cultures put their own spin on them, and scrambled eggs can be a versatile add-on to many other dishes. However, we are going to take a look at how to make the best stand-alone scrambled eggs possible

Ingredients

  • Two large eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a large bowl
  2. Heat up a teaspoon of olive oil in a nonstick pan on meadium heat
  3. Whisk the eggs with a fork or whisk until the yolk and white are thoroughly incorporated
  4. Add eggs to pan
  5. Mix eggs once they start to solidify on the pan
  6. Remove the eggs from the heat at least once while stirring, this is key to having creamier eggs
  7. Once eggs are cooked through but not dry in appearance, add a pinch of salt and mix
  8. Enjoy!
