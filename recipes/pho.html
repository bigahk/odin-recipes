Pho

Pho is a Vietnamese staple that has taken the US by storm

This delicious soup consists of rice noodles, fresh greens, thin slices of meat, and most importantly, hardy beef broth. Its origins are unknown, but it gained significant notariety in the US starting in the mid 2000s. If you are craving something comforting, warm, and outright amazing, this is the perfect recipe for you.
