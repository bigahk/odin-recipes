Arroz Con Pollo

This is a dish enjoyed by many in Central America and in places like Cuba

This delicious soup consists of rice, chicken, achote, lots of seasonings, and a lot of other options such as vienna weiners. Though not as popular as other latin dishes, arroz con pollo is packed with flavor. Lets get into it!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Sazon with Achote
  • Your prefered oil
  • Bay leaf
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Make the rice
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Add it in a pot
  4. Mix it up
  5. Add the seasonings
  6. Enjoy
