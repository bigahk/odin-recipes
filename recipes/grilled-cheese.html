Grilled Cheese

An American Classic

The grilled cheese is a childhood favorite for many in the US. Though simple, it can be divinely comforting and tasty. When paired with tomato soup, its nearly unbeatable!

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Salted Butter
  • Kraft American Cheese

Steps

  1. Coat one side of each slice of bread with a liberal amount of butter
  2. Heat up a pan on medium heat
  3. Place two pieces of cheese on the non-butter-coated side of one of the slices of bread
  4. Place the other slice on top, buttered side facing away from the cheese
  5. Place the sandwich on the pan, toast until the bottom is golden brown
  6. Flip and repeat
  7. Enjoy!
